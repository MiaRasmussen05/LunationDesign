About Us

Who is Lunation Design?

Lunation Design is a one-person operation. However, it is one person who loves what they do and they want to share this with as many as they can. Hard work, passion, creativity, and love are all things they put into every design they make.

How did it all start?

It all started with a small pun. That turned into an idea for a drawing. Now there are multiple stickers just ready for you to choose, and more coming. So jump your way to the stickers library and see if we have just what you are looking for!

What can I do for you?

Found what you are looking for? No? Then send a message to Lunation Design on our contact page, about what you need for a custom job, with all the information you want on your design and see it come to life. Or just want to send them a message, no problem. Lunation Design can't wait to hear from you.
